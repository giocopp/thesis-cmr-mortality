=== WHY WEEKLY GIVES NULL RESULTS ===

Weekly aggregation smooths out high-frequency weather variation.
FE absorb 46% of weekly SWH variation vs 23% of daily.
After absorbing seasonality, there is insufficient residual SWH
variation at weekly frequency to identify the gradient change.
The daily panel is the correct frequency for this question.
